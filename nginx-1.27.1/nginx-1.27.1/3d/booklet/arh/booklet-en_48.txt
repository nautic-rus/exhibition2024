TRAWLER 48M vessel equipped as a fresh fish trawler with a registration length under 45m capable of fishing with three bottom trawls simultaneously, equipped with 4 trawl winches. The hull is designed with the EndurobowTM bulbous bow shape providing various behavioural benefits from the design, including increased comfort for a crew upto 16 persons. The processing deck is a completely open space with no support pillars.


BASIC INFORMATION:
- overall length	48.10 m
-width	14.00m
- number of crew	16 people
- max draft	5,75 m
- vessel speed	13 knots

FISH SPECIES:
- cod
- pollack
- haddock
- flatfish
- red fish


- herring
- mackerel
- squid
- shrimps
- and other types of fish


METHOD OF FISHING:
- The vessel is outfitted for bottom trawling with up to 3 trawls simultaneously and can operate as a shrimp trawler as well.



Fish processing area with an area of 285m2 for the primary processing of the catch.


HOLD CAPACITY:
- fish hold - 630 m3 (approx. 185 tons of fresh fish in plastic tubs)


METHODS AND TYPES OF PROCESSING:
- gutting and cooling of fish for further processing onshore
