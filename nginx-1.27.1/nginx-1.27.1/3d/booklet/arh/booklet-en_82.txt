TRAWLER 82M big freezing processing trawler, designed for maximum fishing efficiency. The design based on the well-established and proven Enduro TM Bow concept for the best seaworthiness in rough seas. This bow shape also allows full use of the limited size of the vessel for the benefit of the crew and the maximum capacity of the fish hold and the vessel as a whole.



BASIC INFORMATION:
- overall length	82.00 m
- width	16.00 m
- number of crew	80 people
- scantling draft	6,30 m
- vessel speed	15 knots

FISH SPECIES:
- cod
- pollack
- haddock
- flatfish


- red fish
- halibut
- shrimp
- and other bottom types of fish


METHOD OF FISHING:
- trawling, with possibility of using 2 trawls




TRAWLER 82m is equipped with a fish processing factory with automatic packaging and palletizing.



PRODUCTON CAPACITY:
- total processing capacity on the ship - 150t / day
- freezing capacity - 100t / day



METHODS AND TYPES OF PROCESSING:
- frozen blocks of fillets
- boneless fillets (with / without skin), fillets with bones (with/without skin), portioned fillets and cuts
- fish h/ g frozen in blocks

HOLD CAPACITY:
- main fish hold - 1565 m3
- combined fish hold - 310 m3




- canned liver
- offal (heads, minced meat) in frozen blocks
- fish meal and fish oil
